Carousel & IG TV Ads
The Goal

Boost brand awareness

Seoul Euro Dental Centre wanted to increase brand awareness among Korean women, particularly those who were highly interested in the health & beauty and in taking care of themselves.

The Solution

Influencer ads at the fore

To expand the audience for its latest ad campaign and raise awareness in the female demographic, Seoul Euro Dental Centre decided to run video and carousel ads in Instagram feed, Instagram Stories and Facebook News Feed in its first-ever campaign aimed specifically at Korean women.

Seoul Euro designed the ad content to help female viewers make a connection between the Seoul Euro Dental brand and beauty, and the videos all featured 50-year old Instagram/YouTube influencer Lee Sujin. The ad creative showed young-looking Lee exercising at the gym and with an affable group of girls and sharing her beauty routine, explaining how beauty has pretty teeth, and included messaging that Seoul Euro Dental Centre's immediate implant can help to minimize the fear and pain of anesthesia. Some ads had a Learn More call-to-action button that linked through to the company website.

Seoul Euro Dental showed the ads to Korean women aged 34–55, as well as to those with an interest in health, beauty, lifestyle, and fitness. The company determined the results of the campaign using a Facebook/IG brand lift study, which revealed:

  • 14-point lift in ad recall
  • 7-point lift in brand awareness among those aged 35–44
  • 1.8 million people reached
